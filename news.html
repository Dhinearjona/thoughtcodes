Thoughtcodes Launches Redesigned Website

Alexandria, Virginia – May 10, 2019 – Thoughtcodes announces the release of its newly redesigned website today. Thoughtcodes’s new website features a modern, “mobile-first” design that is responsive to both desktop and mobile users. This website, built using open source technology, boasts an updated and simpler navigation menu, as well as all new pages with original content.The site also incorporates multimedia-rich content and integrates with Thoughtcodes’s social media.

Thoughtcodes Awarded Enterprise Reporting Portal Project for USAID

Thoughtcodes was awarded a data analytics and advanced dashboarding project at the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) Knowledge Management Branch using Force.com. Thoughtcodes will develop an “Enterprise Reporting Portal,” or ERP, on Salesforce.com’s cloud platform (Site.com).

Washington, DC Dec 19th, 2018: Thoughtcodes, a global software solutions provider, Microsoft Partner and winner of several Microsoft “Partner of the Year” Awards, presented an event titled “Transform your business through Azure Cloud, Office 365, Dynamics 365 (ERP and CRM)”.
